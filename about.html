About Om Precast Cement Allied Product Industries

Excellence in Precast & Cement Construction Materials Since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Om Precast Cement Allied Product Industries has become a leading manufacturer of precast compound walls, pavers, RCC poles, toilet blocks, and more. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.

We serve clients across Karnataka and beyond, delivering innovative and reliable construction solutions for every need.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable, and high-quality construction materials that empower builders to create lasting structures while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in construction materials, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable practices, while contributing to the development of modern infrastructure.
